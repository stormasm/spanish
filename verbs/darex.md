
#### Emotions or physical reactions with dar and poner

note that we use a noun with dar and an adjective with poner

- me pone contento - it makes (puts) me happy
- me da alegría - it makes me happy - it gives me happiness
- me pone triste - it makes (puts) me sad
- me da tristeza - it makes me sad - it gives me sadness

---

- me da miedo - it makes me scared - it scares me
- me da hambre - it makes me hungry
- me da sed - it makes me thirsty
- me da calor - it makes me hot
- me da frio - it makes me cold
- me da risa - it makes me laugh
- me da pena - it makes me feel sorry
- me da vergüenza - it makes me embarrassed (ashamed)

---

 - tengo miedo - I am scared
 - tengo vergüenza - I am embarrassed (ashamed)
 - tengo hambre - I am hungry
 - tengo sed - I am thirsty

---

- me da pena contigo - I feel sorry for you
- le - he feels sorry for you
- la - she
- nos - we
- les - they
- te da pena contigo - you feel sorry for yourself
- me da felicidad, amor, y un nuevo trabajo - she gives me happiness, love, and a new job.
