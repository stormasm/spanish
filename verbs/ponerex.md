
# Poner

- me pones un cafe - can I have a coffee

---

- eso me pone nervioso - that makes me nervous
- me pone de buen humor - it puts me in a good mood
- me pone de mal humor - it puts me in a bad mood

---

- me pone contento - it makes me happy
- tenso - tense
- triste - sad
- celoso - jealous
- emocionado - excited
