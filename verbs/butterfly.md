## [alcanzar](https://www.spanishdict.com/translate/alcanzar)

- [butterfly](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ua0_D7GxNuc)

### to catch

- Tuve que correr para alcanzar el tren - I had to run to catch the train
- No voy a alcanzar el tren - I am not going to catch the train
- Voy a llegar tarde porque no alcancé el tren de las 3, I am going to be late because I did not catch the 3 o'clock train.

---

Say the following sentence not when you forgot something but when they had already run out of the product that you are talking about.  In other words the store had run out of aguacates.

- No alcancé aguacates
- No alcancé la tienda abierta, no alcancé el banco abierta.
- I didn't make it to the store while it was still open.

### to reach

- alcanzas ese libro, can you reach that book ?
- alcanzas el foco, can you reach the bulb ?
- No, no lo alcanzo, no I can not reach it.

### to not have enough

- No me alcanza para un libro, I don't have enough (money) for a book
- No me alcanza para mi renta, I do not have enough to pay the rent.

### do not have enough of something

- No va a alcanzar la carne, I am not going to have enough meat
- No me alcanzan los huevos, I don't have enough eggs to make the cake

### to reach

- La temperatura alcanzó los 30 degrees, the temperature reached 30 degrees
- A que no me alcanzas, I bet that you can not reach me
