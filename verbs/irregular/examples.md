
### caer

caigo means I fall or I am falling.    
It is also commonly used in phrases meaning "I understand" or to indicate if you are liked by someone.

Here are a few examples:

- "Me caigo de sueño." (I'm falling asleep on my feet/I'm exhausted.)
- "Casi me caigo de la silla." (I almost fell off my chair.)"
- ¿Te caigo bien?" (Do you like me?)"
- ¡Ya caigo!" (Oh, I get it now!/I understand!)
- "A veces caigo, pero nunca me rindo." (I sometimes fall, but I never give up.)
