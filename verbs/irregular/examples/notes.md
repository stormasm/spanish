

### va a estar

- va a estar - its going to be
- va a estar bien - its going to be ok
- va a estar lloviendo - its going to be raining

### va a salir

- va a salir - its going to come out
- va a salir ? - is it going to be released
- va a salir bien - it is going to be ok

##### Note01

Ambas frases transmiten consuelo, pero sutilmente se enfocan en cosas distintas.

"Todo va a salir bien": Se centra en el proceso o resultado final. Implica que, tras superar un obstáculo o evento específico, las cosas se resolverán favorablemente.

"Todo va a estar bien": Se enfoca en el estado de ánimo o la estabilidad. Transmite que, sin importar lo que pase, la situación o la persona encontrarán paz y tranquilidad a largo plazo.

Both phrases offer comfort, but they subtly focus on different things.

"Everything is going to work out": This focuses on the process or the final outcome. It implies that, after overcoming a specific obstacle or event, things will resolve favorably.

"Everything is going to be okay": This focuses on one's state of mind or stability. It conveys that, no matter what happens, the situation or the person will find peace and tranquility in the long run.
