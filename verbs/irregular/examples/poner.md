

Puse is the "yo" (I) form of the verb *poner* (to put/place) in the past tense (preterite). It is highly versatile and is used for physically placing items, putting on clothing, or expressing a change in emotion or state. \[[1](https://ellaverbs.com/spanish-verbs/poner-conjugation/), [2](https://www.spanishdict.com/translate/yo%20me%20puse), [3](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tHiLQ1b0IU4&t=5), [4](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5GcHInADAHY)\]

Physically placing objects

* Yo puse las llaves en la mesa. (I put the keys on the table.)  
* Puse mi maleta en el maletero. (I put my suitcase in the trunk.)  
* Ayer puse los regalos en el desván. (Yesterday I put the gifts in the attic.) \[[1](https://www.clozemaster.com/translate/spa-eng/puse), [2](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tHiLQ1b0IU4&t=5), [3](https://www.clozemaster.com/examples/spa-eng/puse?page=2)\]

Putting on clothing or accessories

* Me puse el abrigo grueso porque hacía frío. (I put on my heavy coat because it was cold.)  
* Me puse mis lentes para leer. (I put on my glasses to read.) \[[1](https://context.reverso.net/translation/spanish-english/puse), [2](https://www.clozemaster.com/examples/spa-eng/puse?page=2)\]

Changing emotions or states

* Me puse nervioso antes de la entrevista. (I got nervous before the interview.)  
* Me puse nervioso antes de la prueba. (I got nervous before the test)
* De pronto me puse muy triste. (I suddenly became very sad.)  
* Me puse rojo de la vergüenza. (I turned red with embarrassment.) \[[1](https://www.clozemaster.com/examples/spa-eng/puse?page=3), [2](https://www.clozemaster.com/examples/spa-eng/puse?page=2)\]

Starting an activity (with "a")

* Después de comer, me puse a limpiar. (After eating, I started cleaning.)  
* Me puse a estudiar para el examen. (I started studying for the exam.) \[[1](https://www.clozemaster.com/examples/spa-eng/puse?page=3), [2](https://www.spanishdict.com/phrases/puse), [3](https://www.spanishdict.com/translate/yo%20me%20puse), [4](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tHiLQ1b0IU4&t=5)\]
