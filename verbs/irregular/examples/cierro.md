Verbs: Example Sentences

"Cierro" is the first-person singular present tense conjugation of *cerrar* (to close), meaning "I close" or "I am closing". It is used to describe an action the speaker is doing or does regularly, such as sealing a door, finishing a task, or closing eyes. \[[1](https://www.spanishdict.com/translate/cierro), [2](https://www.123teachme.com/translated_sentences/sp/cierro), [3](https://lingualinkup.com/spanish-verbs-cerrar-conjugations/), [4](https://www.collinsdictionary.com/sentences/spanish/cierro)\]

Here are examples of sentences with "cierro" categorized by usage:

Common Daily Usage

* Cierro la puerta todos los días. (I close the door every day)  
* Cierro la ventana porque hace frío. (I am closing the window because it is cold)  
* Siempre cierro los ojos cuando escucho música. (I always close my eyes when I listen to music)  
* Cierro la tienda a las nueve. (I close the shop at nine o'clock) \[[1](https://www.spanishdict.com/translate/cierro), [2](https://www.123teachme.com/translated_sentences/sp/cierro), [3](https://lingualinkup.com/spanish-verbs-cerrar-conjugations/), [4](https://www.spanishdict.com/conjugate/cierro)\]

Contextual Examples

* Le agradezco a Mark por su tiempo y cierro mi cuaderno. (I thank Mark for his time and shut my notebook)  
* Si cierro esa puerta, ya sabes lo que pasa después. (If I close that door, you know what happens next)  
* No se preocupe, yo cierro la puerta. (Don't worry, I'll get/close the door)  
* Cierro lentamente un puño, como si algo me lo impidiera. (I slowly close a fist, as if something was preventing me) \[[1](https://www.spanishdict.com/translate/cierro), [2](https://www.spanishdict.com/translate/yo%20cierro), [3](https://www.collinsdictionary.com/sentences/spanish/cierro)\]

"Me Cierro" (Reflexive/Shut Down)

* Cuando estoy nervioso, me cierro. (When I am nervous, I shut down)  
* No me cierro a probar nuevas formas de gastronomía. (I don't limit myself to trying new types of cuisine) \[[1](https://context.reverso.net/translation/spanish-english/me+cierro)\]
