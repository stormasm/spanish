
### Irregular Verbs


- conocer - know people and places

- dar - give
- decir - say, tell
- estar - be, location, state
- hacer - do, make
- ir - go

- poder - to be able, can
- poner - put, place
- querer - want, love
- saber - know information
- salir - leave, go out

- ser - be, permanent, essential trait
- tener - have, possess
- traer - bring
- venir - come
- ver - see
