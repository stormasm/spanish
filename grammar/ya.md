

- Ya voy - I am leaving now
- Ya comiste - Have you already eaten
- Ya no fumamos - We don't smoke anymore
- Ya comprendo - Finally I understand
- Ya lo sé - I already know that
- Ya lo haré - I will do this
- Ya casi termino - I am almost finished
