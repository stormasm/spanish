### Words - Page 09

#### Espanto

##### 1. (fear)   

a. fright   
- ¡Ay! ¡Otto! Me diste un espanto. No sabía que estabas aquí.
- Ah! Otto! You gave me a fright. I didn't know you were here.
- Casi me muero de espanto cuando dijeron que llamaban del hospital.
- I nearly died of fright when they told me they were calling from the hospital.

b. terror   
- Cuando la encontraron tenía una mirada de espanto.
- When they found her, she had a look of terror in her eyes.

c. horror   
- Me da espanto la idea de subirme a un coche con él al volante.
- I feel horror at the idea of getting in a car with him at the wheel.

##### 2. (something unpleasant)

a. terrible   
- El libro me gustó, pero la película es un espanto.
- I liked the book but the film is terrible.

b. awful   
- ¡Qué espanto de restaurante!
- What an awful restaurant!
