
### Number of Words in the Spanish Language

The Spanish language contains approximately 88,000 to 100,000 dictionary entries in its standard form, with the Royal Spanish Academy's Diccionario de la lengua española listing around 93,000 words.  When including regionalisms, Americanisms, and technical terms, estimates suggest the total vocabulary could reach 150,000 words.

- Dictionary Count: Major dictionaries like the Diccionario del español actual include about 100,000 entries, while the Royal Spanish Academy (RAE) recognizes over 93,000 lemmas.
- Native Speaker Vocabulary: An educated native Spanish speaker typically has an active vocabulary of 30,000 to 50,000 words, though they may recognize up to 40,000 passively.
- Comparative Size: Spanish has roughly half the vocabulary of English, which contains over 250,000 to 470,000 words depending on the dictionary cited.
- Core Usage: Only about 1,500 to 2,000 words are needed to cover approximately 80% to 90% of everyday conversations and texts
