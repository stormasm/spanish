


### Number 4: Adjective Word Order

`At the 9 minute mark`

Adjectives usually come after the Noun

There are 2 adjectives that break the Rule

#### Bueno

Means the same thing whether it comes before or after the noun.

- un buen amigo
- un amigo bueno

If bueno comes before a masculine noun you are going to want to drop the "O"
