
## Tu amor me da paz

Nella's song "Tu Amor Me Da Paz" (Your Love Gives Me Peace), a duet with Marco Mares, is a sweet declaration of finding calm and belonging in a lover's presence, with lyrics about their eyes, smile, and skin bringing comfort, feeling like home, and a desire for endless closeness, really capturing that peaceful, intimate vibe of being truly loved and accepted.
