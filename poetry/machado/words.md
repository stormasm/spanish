### El Tren

##### cofias de lino - linen veil

Is a coif a veil ?

No, a coif is not a veil. A coif is a close-fitting cap (typically made of linen or silk) that covers the top, back, and sides of the head, often tied under the chin.  It serves as a foundational undergarment or base layer for headwear.

A veil is a separate piece of fabric, often transparent or decorative, that is pinned or draped over the coif.  Historically, women wore the coif to contain their hair and maintain modesty, then covered it with a veil, hood, or hat for public appearance. In modern contexts, the coif is primarily worn by Catholic nuns under their veils
